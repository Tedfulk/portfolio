SkyclanFlashcards

Who's up for a review sess?

Flashcards is a demonstration of using Python and Django to help students study for their upcoming exam. This project I worked on was with a team of peers. My goal was to really uderstand what the CRUD functionality was. I wrote function based views in this project to add, edit, and delete decks and their cards. The relationship between the deck in the card was modeled through a foreign key relationship on the card model. Many concepts were learned in this project and I'm thankful for my professor to push me to learn new things.
